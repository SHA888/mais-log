# Synthesis

Longer pieces, 1500–3000 words. Each synthesizes patterns across several
weeks of notes.

Synthesis pieces are where weekly learning starts to take shape. They are
not yet authoritative — that's what essays are for — but they are positions
I am willing to be held to until I write a revision note saying otherwise.

## Cadence

Monthly. One canonical synthesis post per month, ideally.

## Format conventions

- Filenames: `YYYY-MM-slug.md`
- Front matter: `title`, `date`, `description`, optional `tags`
- Length: 1500–3000 words

## Editing policy

**Append-only with revision notes.** Once published, substantive changes are
documented in a `## Revisions` section at the bottom of the post:

```markdown
## Revisions

- **2026-08-15** — Added discussion of indirect prompt injection in §3,
  after reading Greshake et al. more carefully. Original §3 stands above
  the addition, marked with a note.
```

Typo fixes do not require revision notes; substantive changes do.
