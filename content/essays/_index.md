# Essays

Long pieces, 5000+ words. The core writing of this site.

By design, essays are **tri-lens** — A (Attack surface), M (Mechanics), and
C (Consequence) all present, even when the weight across the three is
uneven. An essay that doesn't terminate in C is a piece that hasn't reached
the standard of this track.

Essays are positions I am willing to defend until I write a substantive
revision. They are the artifacts that, over years, may become a reference
at least for future-me.

## Cadence

Quarterly. Four essays per year is the target, not a quota.

## Format conventions

- Filenames: `YYYY-QN-slug/index.md` (page-as-directory, for posts that may
  carry diagrams, figures, or supplementary files)
- Front matter: `title`, `date`, `description`, `tags`
- Length: 5000+ words

## Editing policy

**Append-only with revision notes.** Same discipline as synthesis pieces,
applied more strictly. The original published text remains visible;
substantive changes appear in a `## Revisions` section with date and
rationale.

If an essay's central thesis becomes wrong, I write a new essay rather than
rewriting the old one. The old essay stays as part of the trajectory.
